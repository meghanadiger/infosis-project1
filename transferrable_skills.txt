Transferable skills are skills that are relevant and helpful across different areas of life. Also called portable skills, they include all skills and abilities that can be transferable depending on where they are being transferred to and from. As a web developer, it is quiet important to use your current skills as a raw clue and tyr finding solutions for exising problems and challenges. Talents and abilities you can take from job to job, or use in multiple careers, are called transferable skills.
